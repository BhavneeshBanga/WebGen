Our Story

Since 2015, we've been serving the most delicious and authentic Indian kulchas in the heart of the city. Our family recipe, passed down through generations, combines traditional flavors with a modern twist.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients - fresh butter, aromatic spices, and premium flour - to create the perfect fluffy, flavorful kulchas that keep our customers coming back for more.
